Life And Baseball

By: Chris Gress

Life and baseball are nearly the same.

The Highs, The Lows.

The Balls, The Strikes.

You live, one day at a time.

You play the game, one pitch at a time.

The Past is gone, but not forgotten.

The Future looks bright, but is not guaranteed.

Life, is about the Little Things.

Baseball, is about the fundamentals.

In life you stay humble, but not timid.

In baseball you stay confident, but not cocky.
